# Prior-Art Boundary

Distinction Theory does not claim priority over the existence of the following concepts:
- distinction / indication
- information as difference
- autopoiesis
- Markov blankets
- Free Energy Principle
- Landauer erasure
- Bekenstein bounds
- horizon thermodynamics
- decoherence
- topological phases
- transfer entropy

The claimed contribution of DT is the dependency chain connecting finite distinction, boundary maintenance, capacity deficit, approximation, corrective complexity, dissipation pressure, active pruning, collapse, and invariant-supported persistence.
