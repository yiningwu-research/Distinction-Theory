# FDS-X1 — Pre-Registered Prediction Registry

**Purpose:** Pre-register high-risk DT/FDS bridge claims before Euclid DR1.

A pre-registered prediction is not a claim of certainty. It is a public commitment to a statement, its dependencies, and its failure conditions before the relevant outcome is known.

## Prediction X1-A — Horizon-Maintenance Energy Scale

**Statement.** If cosmological horizons are finite distinguishability boundaries maintained by finite observers, the maintenance cost has characteristic scale rho_HM ~ H^2 M_Pl^2, consistent with the observed dark-energy density.

**Dependencies.** FDS-T1-001 (finite observer projection); FDS-T1-002 (distinguishability budget); general relativity as background.

**Not claimed.** This does not derive the exact numerical value of Lambda or predict Omega_Lambda with precision.

**Failure condition (strong).** Robust high-precision measurement ruling out any dynamical dark energy consistent with horizon-scale physics.

**Failure condition (weak).** If w(z) is measured to be exactly -1 with no evolution, the non-dynamical version is supported but the dynamical version is demoted.

---

## Prediction X1-B — Non-Phantom Tendency

**Statement.** If horizon-maintenance cost drives late-time acceleration, the effective equation of state tends toward w = -1 from above (non-phantom), with possible mild evolution at low redshift.

**Dependencies.** X1-A.

**Not claimed.** This does not predict the exact w(z) or its time derivative.

**Failure condition.** Robust detection of w < -1 (phantom) at any statistically significant confidence level.

---

## Demotion Protocol

If X1-A fails observationally:
- X1 is demoted from "active high-risk bridge" to "falsified high-risk claim".
- FDS-T1 (finite observer budget) is not affected.
- FDS-P0 registry is updated to reflect X1 failure.

If X1-B fails (phantom detected):
- X1-A (the energy scale claim) is not automatically falsified.
- X1-B is demoted or quarantined.
- A phantom-compatible extension may be developed as a separate claim.
